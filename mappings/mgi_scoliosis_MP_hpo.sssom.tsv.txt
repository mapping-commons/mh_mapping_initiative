#mapping_set_title: Phenotypes for Scoliosis models									
"#mapping_set_description: Phenotypes are the set of Mammalian Phenotype ontology (MP) terms that are annotated to mouse genotypes annotated as models of the human disease Scoliosis (DOID:0060249) or its child term idiopathic scoliosis (DOID:0060250). Genotypes and annotated MP terms were extracted from MouseMine (7/28/22) using the templates and list functions. Use of skos mappings terms: exact match: MP term is the same as the HPO term, ignoring that the HP term applies only to humans; broad match: MP term covers more than the HPO term; narrow match: MP term is more restircted than the HPO term, ignoring the species restriction; related match: MP term and HPO term share some similarity but would not have a parent-child relation in a shared ontology"
